# Business Notes

The old system is used by the operations team to manage staff evaluation records and review reports.

旧系统主要用于员工评估和巡检记录管理。业务部门希望新系统保留原有的员工、部门、小组、评估记录和审核流程，但界面和权限需要重新梳理。

The president account can view all companies and departments.

总裁账号可以查看所有公司、部门和小组的数据。

Company admins usually manage users and records within their own company.

公司管理员通常负责本公司的人员、评估记录和权限配置。

Department managers review records for their own department, but it is unclear whether they can view records from other departments.

部门主管一般只复核本部门记录，但旧系统里是否能跨部门查看不太确定。

Group leaders submit evaluation notes for their group members.

组长负责提交本组成员的表现记录。

Some permissions were configured manually in the old system, so the new system should make role and data scope rules more explicit.

旧系统的权限配置比较混乱，新系统需要把角色权限和数据范围设计清楚。

The new system should preserve auditability. It should be possible to know who submitted, reviewed, approved, rejected, or edited a record.

新系统需要保留操作留痕，能够看到谁提交、谁审核、谁通过、谁退回、谁编辑过记录。
